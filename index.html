$TRIAS
$TRIAS is the functional token and value unit of the Trias Layer '-1' network, serving as the core link for the security mechanism and ecological development of the Trias network. All ecosystem built on the Trias network require $TRIAS to maintain it's operations. $TRIAS is also a representation of the value within the Trias ecosystem, with numerous use cases throughout the Trias network architecture and ecosystem. The Trias economic model is constructed around $TRIAS, aiming to incentivize all parties to collectively drive the development and sustained operation of the ecosystem.
